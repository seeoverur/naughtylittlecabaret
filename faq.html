Performance Arts FAQ

Answers to common questions about our Chicago-based cabaret and variety performances.

Our shows are cabaret-style variety productions featuring a rotating mix of dance, burlesque-inspired acts, aerial artistry, vocal performances, character comedy, drag entertainment, and specialty skills. Each night is curated to feel dynamic and theatrical, offering something new for returning guests.

Our performances are designed for mature audiences. While the content focuses on theatrical artistry and entertainment, guests should be 21+ unless otherwise noted for special events.

Most productions run approximately 90 to 120 minutes, including brief intermissions. Special themed nights or guest showcases may vary slightly in duration.

Our productions are hosted at partnered venues throughout Chicago. Specific locations are listed on each event page along with directions and parking information.

Door sales may be available if the event is not sold out. However, advance ticket purchases are strongly recommended as seating is limited.

Our cast includes both Chicago-based artists and touring performers from across the country. We aim to showcase a mix of established talent and emerging creatives.
