Meet the Cast

Our Chicago-based cabaret troupe features a rotating collective of performers across dance, aerial arts, vocals, drag, comedy, and specialty performance disciplines. Each artist brings their own style, story, and stage presence.

Scarlett Monroe

Aerial & Dance Artist

Scarlett blends contemporary dance with aerial performance, creating dramatic silhouettes and fluid transitions. Her stage presence combines elegance with bold theatrical storytelling.

Vincent Noir

Cabaret Vocalist

With a background in live theater and jazz performance, Vincent delivers powerhouse vocals and charismatic emceeing. His performances channel classic cabaret charm with modern flair.

Lola Stardust

Drag Artist & Host

Lola brings quick wit, glamour, and high-energy crowd interaction to every show. Known for sparkling costumes and playful humor, she keeps audiences engaged from curtain to curtain call.

Aria Vale

Pole & Movement Artist

Aria combines strength, balance, and precision in her pole and floorwork choreography. Her performances focus on artistry, control, and dynamic musical interpretation.

Theo Blaze

Comedy & Variety Performer

Theo specializes in character-driven comedy and physical performance. His acts weave humor, improvisation, and theatrical timing into unforgettable moments on stage.

Celeste Orion

Specialty Performance Artist

Celeste incorporates prop work, visual illusions, and theatrical movement into her sets. Her performances are immersive, expressive, and visually captivating.

A Rotating Chicago Collective

Our cast evolves throughout the season, featuring both Chicago-based artists and touring performers. Each show introduces new talent, ensuring no two nights feel the same.
