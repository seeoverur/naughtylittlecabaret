Who Can Attend?

Classes are open to adults of varying experience levels. Whether you are new to performance arts or looking to sharpen existing skills, our instructors create a welcoming and structured learning environment.

What to Bring

Comfortable movement attire, water, and any specialty footwear relevant to your discipline. Additional requirements may be shared after registration.
