The Show

Velvet & Smoke Cabaret is a Chicago performance troupe crafting a modern, variety-forward cabaret with burlesque flair. Our stage celebrates artistry across disciplines—from precision dance and comedic bits to powerhouse vocals and jaw-dropping acrobatics.

Each program is curated like a mixtape: a little glamour, a little mischief, and a lot of talent. If you love shows that feel alive, interactive, and unpredictable, you’ll fit right in.
